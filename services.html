What services HAPPINESS MEDICAL CENTER offers?

Explore our wide range of health services designed to improve your quality of life with expert care and modern facilities.

Obstetrics

Obstetric ultrasonography is routinely used for dating the gestational age of a pregnancy from the size of the fetus, determine the number of fetuses and placentae, evaluate for an ectopic pregnancy and first trimester bleeding, the most accurate dating being in first trimester before the growth of the foetus has been significantly influenced by other factors.

Gynecologist

A dedicated gynecologist is in charge of this clinic where different cases as regard female body and their organs/reproductive system are being attended to. We provide care for various obstetrics & gynecology case such as fibroid, ovarian cyst, perimenopause, contraception counseling, hormonal imbalance, infertility, abnormal uterine bleeding, dysmenorrhea, vaginal infection, endometriosis, etc. We provide care before, during and after child birth.

Surgrical Services

It is a medical procedure involving an incision with instruments; performed to repair damage or arrest disease in a living body. Here at Blue Cross Hospital, we have highly skilled and experienced specialists who provide extensive surgical procedures., These procedures ranges from minor, intermediate or major surgeries which could either be invasive, minimally invasive or non-invasive; also whether the surgery is elective or emergency, our skilled surgeons with the scope and competence to perform diverse surgical interventions and operations are always available.

Internal Medicine Services

We purvey services such as prevention, diagnosis and treatment of adulthood related diseases. Our responsibility is to make available long term comprehensive care, managing both common and complex illnesses. Our esteemed team of visiting consultants, general practitioners, pharmacist, nurses and other categories of medical staff.

Emergency Services

We are dedicated to deliver professional and swift emergency medical services to every client that needs it. We provide a 24-hour ambulance service to shuttle patients to and fro their destinations. An ambulance is a medically equipped vehicle which transports patients to treatment facilities, such as hospitals. Typically, out-of-hospital medical care is provided to the patient. Ambulance buses are discussed at greater length in their own article

Orthopedic

This service provides consultation and treatment for all general orthopedic ailments. The clinic is managed by a visiting consultant who is skilled and vast in the management of diseases and injuries affecting the muscular-skeletal system, which in turn improves the living condition of the client. Areas of specialties include, but not limited to: Arthritis, Infections of bones and joints, Osteoporosis and related bones diseases, Joint pains, Etc.

Medical Diagnostics

The diagnostic department comprise of the Laboratory and Radiology sections. Both sections are manned by a team of highly specialized Scientist and Radiographer. LABORATORY The laboratory offers international standard services that serve the need of outpatient, inpatient, as well as the emergency departments either within or without the hospital premise. Our Radiographers are well-trained employees that deliver adequate care and are also dedicated to the safety and comfort of our clients as they undergo diagnostic radiology services.

Pharmacy

The pharmacy engage in total pharmaceutical care which is a patient-centered, outcomes oriented pharmacy practice with a pharmacist in-charge who coordinates the affairs of the department. Experienced technicians working with the Pharmacist to provide adequate counsel to every patient on how to take their medications give detail information about their drugs and advise against possible side effects. The Pharmacy opens for 24hours and it services both in-patient and out-patient respectively.

Health CheckUp

Blue Cross Hospital has diverse vital medical assessment initiative that is committed to helping our client both corporate and individual move on with their life objectives with resolved peace of mind and zero worries whatsoever. The comprehensive health check done includes but not limited to Diabetes check, BMI check, Blood pressure check, hematology profile, thyroid function test, liver function test, etc.
